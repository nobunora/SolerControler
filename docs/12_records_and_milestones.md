# Records and Milestones

## When to Write a Record

- Each milestone.
- After risky refactor steps.
- After release gates.
- After useful blockers.

## What to Capture

- changed files
- why
- commands
- results
- limits
- risks
- next step

## Record Style

- Short.
- Factual.
- Summarize logs.
- Say why a command could not run.
- Say what still needs human confirmation.

## Suggested Location

- `docs/records/`
- `docs/08_report_template.md`
